Instructions

Now accepting applications for Fall 2016.
All applications must be submitted by March 1, 2016
Decisions will be made by April 30, 2016.

An applicant for admission must submit the following:

Step 1 - Application Form

Complete and submit the application form and supporting documents to the Department of Biological Sciences (for address, please see step 6).

Step 2 - Recommendation letters

Request that three recommendation forms be submitted directly to the Department of Biological Sciences (for address see step 6).

Recommendation forms should be completed by faculty members or employers.

Step 3 - GRE and TOEFL

Provide unofficial copies of your General GRE scores (Biology GRE scores will be considered). Applicants whose language of instruction for their higher education was not English are required to provide unofficial copies of TOEFL scores. International students whose language of instruction was English must provide proof from their degree-granting institution.

Step 4 - Additional documents

Provide:

1. Unofficial copies of all your University transcripts.

2. A statement of purpose.

3. A current resume.

4. Signed permission to contact references.

Step 5 - Interview

An interview will be scheduled for selected applicants after receipt of all academic documents and preliminary review of all application materials.

About

The San José State University Consortium for Stem Cell Internships in Laboratory-based Learning (SCILL) Program provides coursework towards a Master's degree in the Department of Biological Sciences (Master of Arts or Master of Biotechnology) at San Jose State University and a 12-month, full-time internship in stem cell research in one of four host institutions.

CIRM

Bridges CIRM 1.0 funded the SJSU SCILL Consortium in 2009 with $1.73m, was renewed with additional funding ($1.82m in 2012 and $0.6m in 2014) with the mission to generate a professional workforce that will enter stem cell research.
